Birtha (settlement) — ܒܝܪܗܓܝܟ

i

Place URI: http://syriaca.org/place/50

A village on the Euphrates north of Mabbug and south of Samosata.

Names

  • ܒܝܪܗܓܝܟ
  • Birtha 1
  • ܒܺܝܪܰܗܓܺܝܟ 2
  • بيره جك 3
  • Biricik 4
