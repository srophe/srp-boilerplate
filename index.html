Welcome to the The Syriac Gazetteer

Editors: Thomas A. Carlson (Princeton University) and David A. Michelson (Vanderbilt University)

The Syriac Gazetteer is a growing reference work concerning places relevant to Syriac Studies, currently containing articles about more than 2000 places. The Index page is an alphabetic index (available in either English or Syriac) of all places contained in the gazetteer. The About page offers a scholarly introduction to the gazetteer and its methodology as well as information about editors and contributors. The Help page contains links to documentation, editorial guidelines, and technical definitions. The Search function is not presently enabled, but will be soon!

The Syriac Gazetteer is one of the core reference works published by Syriaca.org as part of the Syriac Reference Portal. Funding for The Syriac Gazetteer has been possible through the generous support of the The Andrew W. Mellon Foundation, The International Balzan Prize Foundation, and the National Endowment for the Humanities. The institutional sponsors of The Syriac Gazetteer are Vanderbilt University, Princeton University, and Beth Mardutho: The Syriac Institute.
